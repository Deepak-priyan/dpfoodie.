Cancellation

As a general rule you shall not be entitled to cancel your order once placed. You may choose to cancel your order only within one-minute of the order being placed by you. However, subject to your previous cancellation history, Foodie reserves the right to deny any refund to you pursuant to a cancellation initiated by you even if the same is within one-minute.

Terms and Condition

Order Acceptance: When you place an order through Foodie, it is subject to acceptance by the restaurant or vendor. We are not responsible for any errors in the availability or pricing of menu items.

Prices of menu items and delivery fees are displayed on the platform. We strive to ensure accuracy, but prices may change, and errors may occur. In such cases, we will contact you to confirm the order.

Payment: All orders must be paid in full via the available payment methods on the platform. You agree to provide valid payment details and authorize us to charge the total order amount to your chosen payment method.

FAQs

  • Is there a minimum order value?

We have no minimum order value and you can order for any amount.

  • Do you charge for delivery?

Delivery fee varies from city to city and is applicable if order value is below a certain amount. Additionally, certain restaurants might have fixed delivery fees. Delivery fee (if any) is specified on the 'Review Order' page.

  • How long do you take to deliver?

Standard delivery times vary by the location selected and prevailing conditions. Once you select your location, an estimated delivery time is mentioned for each restaurant.

Partnership

  • What are the mandatory documents needed to list my restaurant on Foodie?

- Copies of the below documents are mandatory
- FSSAI Licence OR FSSAI Acknowledgement
- Pan Card
- GSTIN Certificate
- Cancelled Cheque OR bank Passbook
- Menu

  • After I submit all documents, how long will it take for my restaurant to go live on Foodie?

After all mandatory documents have been received and verified it takes upto 7-10 working days for the onboarding to be completed and make your restaurant live on the platform.

  • What is this one time Onboarding fees? Do I have to pay for it while registering?

his is a one-time fee charged towards the system & admin costs incurred during the onboarding process. It is deducted from the weekly payouts after you start receiving orders from Foodie.
